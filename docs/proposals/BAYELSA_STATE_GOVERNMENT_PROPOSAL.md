## The Bayelsa Context

### Current Challenges

#### Youth Unemployment
- Estimated **35-40%** unemployment among 18-35 year olds
- Limited formal sector opportunities
- Brain drain to Lagos, Port Harcourt, abroad
- Underutilized entrepreneurial potential

#### Informal Economy
- **80%+** of commerce is informal/unregistered
- Cash-based transactions limit growth
- No consumer protection mechanisms
- Tax revenue leakage

#### Digital Divide
- Low adoption of digital payment platforms
- Limited e-commerce activity despite smartphone penetration
- Lack of localized digital solutions
- Trust deficit in online transactions
